Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj (1630–1680) was a great Indian warrior king and the founder of the Maratha Empire in western India. Born on 19 February 1630 at the Shivneri Fort in Maharashtra, he was the son of Shahaji Bhosale and Jijabai. Inspired by his mother, Shivaji developed strong values and a love for his culture and land from a young age.
